How it works

Information gathering

To get the most of out of your comprehensive financial planning journey and a suitable action plan, please fill in as many of the applicable fields as possible to the best of your ability. Every piece of information matters as the quality of the evaluation is very much dependent on the information you provide us.

Get your 3-in-1 Analysis Report

You can acess your 3-in-1 Analysis Report on your MoneyOwl Account Dashboard within 1-2 working days. The report will comprise:
  • Your Financial Health Check
  • Your Protection Needs Analysis
  • Your Retirement and Children’s Education Plan

Face-to-Face: Client Adviser Time (2 hrs) and Implementation Plan

When you are ready, our Client Adviser will meet you for a fuller consultation of up to two hours, where we can discuss your Financial Health report, refine your analysis and goals and customise your plan further. We will also help you to implement the plan through specific product recommendations, if you choose to do so.

Prompt: Review

Financial planning is an ongoing process. We will prompt you to review your plan once a year. For a limited period, our clients will not be charged for the first-year review.
