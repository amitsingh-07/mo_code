How to get your SRS account
You can sign up for a SRS account with any of these 3 banks - DBS, OCBC, and UOB via their website.
How to invest your SRS with MoneyOwl
Once you have your SRS account ready, you can select SRS as your portfolio funding method and fund your portfolio using your SRS account.
