Investment Pre-requisites

Before you invest using your CPF-OA monies, it is important to assess that you have the knowledge in investing in Unlisted Specified Investment Products (SIPs). You should have also set up your CPF Investment Account.
Please complete the 2 steps below:
Step 1: Complete Customer Knowledge Assessment (CKA)
Step 2: Fill in CPF Investment Account details
Please ensure you have entered the correct account information.
